RPG Cheatsheet Template

People are easily annoyed when quests broke without warning. Most online guides are served in static pages, making progress tracking difficult.

Maybe an interactive template with unified CSV sheet format can help.

Priority levels:

  • HIGH for questline-related missable actions.
  • MEDIUM for standalone missible events.
  • LOW for standard events available any time throughout the main quest.
  • NG for discouraged acts and choices which trigger negative endings or end quests early.

Manually edit cheatsheet.csv for data, labels.json for label definition and emoji mapping.

Double-click on items to tick them off. Your progress is saved locally.
